About Me

My Introduction

I am a Software Engineer (Cloud & DevOps/ServiceNow) with 4+ years of experience specializing in AWS Cloud, CI/CD automation, containerization, and ITSM integrations. My expertise lies in designing and implementing scalable cloud architectures, automating deployments, and optimizing IT service management using ServiceNow. At Ensono Technologies, I have delivered seamless ServiceNow integrations with external systems using Python and AWS services (Lambda, DynamoDB, SQS, API Gateway, S3), streamlining incident, request, change, and problem management workflows. Previously, as a DevOps Engineer at Factals Infotech, I provisioned and managed highly available AWS environments, automated CI/CD pipelines with Jenkins, deployed containerized applications on ECS, and implemented monitoring solutions with Prometheus, Grafana. With certifications in AWS Cloud Practitioner, AWS Solutions Architect Associate, and ServiceNow Service Bridge, I bring both depth and adaptability in cloud-native and ITSM domains. I enjoy building automation-driven solutions that improve efficiency, enhance performance, and deliver business value. Outside of work, I am passionate about exploring new technologies, solving complex integration challenges, and contributing to innovative projects that push the boundaries of cloud and ITSM automation.
